The ACTive Cricket Academy is designed for young cricketers to enhance their cricket skills and general conditioning while training with peers and creating friendships.

What we offer

Community coaching courses

Manitoba Cricket along with other provinces and Territory Associations have embarked on an Industry first by offering Community (Level 1) coaching courses in 2018-19 to the grassroots cricket community!

Representative Coach

The Representative Coaching Course is designed for people coaching players or teams at a representative level or pathway programs. The course will extend your skills as a coach, learning how to progress players to enhance their skills and reach their goals.

High Performance Coach

The Cricket Manitoba High Performance Coaching Program is registered with the Canadian Sports Commission and is Manitoba's flagship coach education program. Successful applicants are likely to be active coaches that have completed the Representative Coach .
